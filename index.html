Overview

Portable, non-invasive blood pressure monitor by Ramsey Medical, Inc.

Clinical Use

Measures systolic, diastolic, and mean pressures, plus heart rate in small animals.

Use

Select species and site, attach the cuff, and press Start.

Attachments

Cuff, power cord, optional SD card, stand, and cover.

QuickCuffs

Durable, flexible cuffs with Velcro-free closure for smooth use.

Cleaning

Wipe with 70% alcohol, air-dry cuffs, never submerge the device.

Maintenance

Check for leaks, charge battery, and calibrate yearly.

How It Works

The cuff inflates and detects arterial pulsations to calculate blood pressure values.
