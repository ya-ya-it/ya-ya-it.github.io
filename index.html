TropiClean Fresh Breath Gel

A client’s perspective on pet dental care

About the Product

I chose this product because of the bright colors of its package, form of application, and the promotional images. The product label states that it “removes plaque and tartar” and helps support fresh breath. The form of application is a gel, so you directly apply it to the dog's teeth without the need to brush. For owners who are having trouble brushing his dog's teeth, this product is most striking. The packaging shows a smiling, healthy dog with clean teeth, creating a positive association.

Marketing Points

Removes plaque and tartar

Supports fresh breath

Natural ingredients

No dyes or sulfates

Cruelty-free

Results in 30 days

TropiClean is the manufacturer of the product, a company that is producing pet grooming and oral care products. Their marketing focuses on natural ingredients, mint flavor, no dyes or sulfates, and promises visible improvement in 30 days.

The directions are fairly simple. According to the manufacturer, you put two drops on both sides of the dog's mouth daily. Licking spreads the gel to cover all tooth and gum surfaces.

Additional Recommendation

When I asked for general oral health recommendations, the associate suggested using this gel together with antler bones. According to them, the bones provide mechanical action of cleaning and are safe for use. To prevent tooth damage, they recommended starting with split antler bones, as they are softer, and then moving on to regular ones.

Conclusion

From my perspective as a client, I was interested in the product because it is so easy to apply, there is a promise of results with images showing before and after, and the store associate recommended it. As a pet owner with no formal training in animal oral health, I was convinced by those claims. My dog doesn’t like having their teeth brushed, so removing that extra stress is very important to me.
